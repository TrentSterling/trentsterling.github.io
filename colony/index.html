Dwarf Workers

Hire dwarves to generate resources passively. Feed them, equip them, and watch your colony grow. Workers need food to survive.

Building System

7+ building types with exponential scaling. Each level costs more but produces more. Mines, farms, forges, and temples.

5 branching tech trees with prerequisites. Unlock new buildings, resource types, multipliers, and automation.
