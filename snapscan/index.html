Everything you need

Capture, organize, and search thousands of screenshots. All in one app.

📷

Built-in Screen Capture

Global hotkeys for fullscreen (Shift+PrtSc) and region select (Alt+PrtSc). Auto-copies to clipboard, plays a sound, and flows straight into the AI pipeline.

🧠

AI Vision Labeling

Gemma 3 27B analyzes every screenshot locally, generating descriptions, categories, tags, and OCR text. No API keys needed.

🔍

Instant Search

Full-text search across AI descriptions, OCR text, filenames, and tags. Find that screenshot from 6 months ago in seconds.

📂

Auto-Categorization

Screenshots are automatically sorted into categories: Dev, Gaming, Social, Browse, Work, Creative, Media, Chat, and more.

🔒

100% Local & Private

Everything runs on your machine. Your screenshots never leave your computer. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking.

⚡

Fast Scanning

Indexes 10,000+ screenshots in under a minute. Generates thumbnails, hashes, and metadata in parallel.

📦

Collections & Projects

Organize screenshots into collections and projects. Bulk tag, favorite, or move hundreds of images at once.

How it works

1

Install & Launch

Download the single .exe, install Ollama, and launch SnapScan.

2

Capture or Scan

Use built-in hotkeys to capture new screenshots, or scan existing folders to index what you already have.

3

AI Labels

Gemma 3 analyzes each image locally, adding descriptions, tags, categories, and OCR text.

4

Find Anything

Search, filter, and browse your entire screenshot library. Every image is labeled and searchable.
