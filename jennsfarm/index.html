Features

A cozy farming game running entirely in your browser.

▒

World Curvature Shader

Animal Crossing-style rolling world effect. A vertex shader bends geometry away from the camera, making the world look like a small planet.

☘

Farming Loop

Till soil, plant seeds, water crops, wait for growth, harvest. Sell at market for coins. Buy more seeds. Expand your farm.

♬

Procedural Audio

All sound effects synthesized at runtime via Web Audio API. Bird chirps by day, cricket sounds at night. No audio files.

★

Procedural Textures

Grass, soil, water, buildings - all generated via Canvas 2D. Pixel-art style with noise variation. Zero external assets.

⚙

Auto-Save

localStorage auto-save triggers after every action. Close the tab, come back later. Your farm is exactly where you left it.

☼

Day/Night Cycle

Sun arcs across the sky. Colors shift through dawn, noon, sunset, and night. Atmospheric lighting with hemisphere lights.
